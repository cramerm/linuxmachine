What is a Progressive Dinner?

Progressive Dinner has as many Courses as guests. Every Course is hosted at another guest's choosing. Depending on the Dinner's theme, each guest prepares their course for the other guests. Say a Dinner has 5 participants and has an American style theme. Then the participants would go to member 1's house or place for an appetizer
